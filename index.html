Dr Nemat Sokhandan

Plant Pathologist

Research Scientist & Plant Disease Specialist

I am a Plant Pathologist and Biotechnologist with extensive experience in plant disease diagnosis, biosecurity, crop protection, research, and agricultural extension. Holding a PhD in Plant Pathology from the University of Sydney, I provide evidence-based consulting services to agricultural businesses, research organisations, government agencies, and growers across Australia.


My expertise includes fungal, bacterial, viral, and nematode diseases, disease risk assessment, biosecurity preparedness, integrated disease management, and scientific reporting. I work closely with clients to deliver practical solutions that protect crop productivity and support sustainable agricultural systems.
